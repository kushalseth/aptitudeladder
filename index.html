About Us


Aptitude Ladder is a professional training institute offering the best quality and customized training in the field of aptitude for different tests conducted by the companies in college campus for recruitment purpose. Aptitude Ladder inspires people to learn and grow step by step steadily, making them realize their true potential so that they become successful in life.


Our approach is to mentor, motivate and help the students in their educational journey to achieve their dreams. We strongly believe in bringing self-awareness in student and enable them to choose the right path in their career.

Our Team


Our trainers are first mentors and then a pool of knowledge in their respective fields with at least 8 years of experience. They all come from strong academic background of B.tech, M.tech and MBA but more appreciable is their way of imparting the knowledge. They have innovative ways of teaching and are always strived to give the students the best. Our holistic teaching methodology coupled with quality classroom delivery make learning a great experience.


SERVICES

What we offer

We provide training in different institutes in the area of Quantitative Aptitude and Reasoning, Verbal Ability and Personal Interview. We have designed our curriculum in such a way so that all relevant knowledge can be given in a specified time period. Following are the topics covered in classrooms.


Quantitative Aptitude

  •   Number

  •   Percentages

  •   Averages and problem of ages

  •   Ratios

  •   Mixtures and allegations

  •   Profit and loss and simple interest and compound interest

  •   Time and work

  •   Pipes and cisterns

  •   Time, speed and distance

  •   Simple equations

  •   Quadratic equations

  •   Basics of P and C

  •   Probability and mensuration

  •   Data Interpretation and data sufficiency

Logical Reasoning

  •   Clocks

  •   Calendars

  •   Venn diagram

  •   Directions

  •   Blood relations and directions

  •   Seating arrangements

  •   Linear/Matrix arrangement

  •   Logic puzzles and selection criteria

Verbal Ability

  •   Reading comprehension - I (Basics)

  •   Reading comprehension - II (Enhanced)

  •   Vocabulary and usage 1

  •   Vocabulary and usage 2

  •   Grammar 1: Verb agreement, tense-based FIB, sentence correction

  •   Grammar 2: Phrasal verbs, verbs, tenses, jumbled paragraphs and logical deductions.

Personal Interview

  •   Workshop

  •   One on one basis



Apart from this Full length tests in the middle and in the end are conducted. Every class is strengthened with an assignment and included test. We do conduct some company specific (e.g. TCS, INFOSYS,L&T, etc.) preparation workshops in the end of training module.
